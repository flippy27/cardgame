# Card Skills - Ideas for Future Implementation

## Implemented
- ✅ Fly - Only flying units can defend
- ✅ Trample - Ignores armor
- ✅ Diagonal Attack - Attacks diagonal slot
- ✅ Melee Range - Front attacks Back

## Defensive Skills
- **Shield** - Reduces next X damage taken (like "Divine Shield" in HS)
- **Regenerate** - Heals X HP at end of turn
- **Taunt** - All opponent attacks target this card (if alive)
- **Evasion** - X% chance to dodge attacks
- **Reflection** - Returns X% of damage taken back to attacker
- **Fortify** - Gains +1 armor per turn
- **Dodge** - Ranged units can't target this card

## Offensive Skills
- **Cleave** - Damages all enemy cards in row
- **Piercing** - Ignores armor (like Trample but different theme)
- **Poison** - Deals X damage per turn to target
- **Bleed** - If attacker survives defense, reduces enemy max health
- **Weak Strike** - Damage reduced by X, but guaranteed hit
- **Stun** - Target can't attack next turn
- **Knockback** - Moves defender to adjacent slot
- **Execute** - Deals bonus damage to damaged cards (% of missing health)
- **Ricochet** - After hitting target, bounces to adjacent card
- **Chain** - Damages all units of same type in enemy board

## Combo/Synergy Skills
- **Rally** - Gives +1/+1 to all Back cards
- **Bloodlust** - Gains +1 attack per card killed this turn
- **Sacrifice** - Kill an allied card to get +2 attack
- **Steal** - Gain +1 armor for each armor on target
- **Leech** - Heal hero equal to damage dealt
- **Drain** - Reduce target's attack by X
- **Mark** - Next card to attack this unit deals +X damage
- **Combo** - Deals bonus damage if another card attacked same target

## Resource/Economy Skills
- **Mana Burn** - Cost enemy X mana when attacking
- **Treasure** - Generate bonus resource when dealing damage
- **Expensive** - Costs more mana but has higher stats (passive)
- **Discount** - Reduces cost to play by 1 per allied card on board

## Movement/Positioning
- **Swap** - Switch positions with target after attack
- **Charge** - Can attack turn it's played
- **Rush** - Can attack but takes reduced damage first turn
- **Teleport** - Move to any empty slot after attacking
- **Phase** - Becomes unblockable (can't be targeted)

## Conditional/Triggered
- **Enrage** - Gains +X attack when damaged
- **Last Stand** - Double damage when alone on board
- **Echo** - Attack twice per turn
- **Momentum** - Increases damage based on consecutive kills
- **Deathwish** - Effect triggers when card dies
- **Bounce** - Returns to hand instead of dying (costs mana)
- **Transform** - Becomes different card type after X turns

## Control/Utility
- **Silence** - Disables target card skills
- **Sleep** - Target skips next attack
- **Freeze** - Target can't move or attack for X turns
- **Curse** - Reduce all stats by X
- **Disarm** - Reduce target attack to 0

## Faction-Specific Ideas
- **Ember** (fire/aggression): Burn, Blazing (attack twice), Heat Wave
- **Forest** (nature/growth): Regenerate, Root (taunted), Vine Wall
- **Storm** (magic/control): Zap (instant damage), Chain Lightning, Stun
- **Shadow** (stealth/sacrifice): Cloak, Corrupt, Drain
- **Holy** (protection/healing): Divine Shield, Martyr (heal when dies), Guardian

## Mechanic Extensions
- **Split Damage** - Divide damage among all cards
- **Distribute** - Choose how to split damage on defense
- **Convert** - Turn armor into attack
- **Upgrade** - Gain +1/+1 each turn
- **Downgrade** - Enemy card gets -1/-1
- **Copy** - Gain stats of target card
- **Sacrifice Chain** - Kill multiple cards for effect

## Passive Abilities (Not Attack-Based)
- **Victory Bonus** - Gain effect if ahead in board control
- **Underdog** - Bonus when low on health
- **Haste** - Act twice per turn
- **Vigilance** - Can attack and block same turn
- **Armor Stacking** - Armor doesn't reset

---

**Notes for Implementation:**
- Skills can be combined (e.g., Fly + Diagonal Attack)
- Some skills need special visual feedback
- Consider balance: strong skills should cost more mana
- Skills should have clear counter-play (e.g., Taunt vs Ranged units)
